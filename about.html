My Story

Hi, my name is Supakorn Laohasongkram. I was born in Bangkok, Thailand. As I write this, I am 27 years old. I graduated from Earlham College in 2010. Ever since college, all I wanted to know is what life is all about, how to rightly live, and what is the solution for making the world a better place. During my time in college, I learned philosophy. After college I ordained as a Buddhist monk for one year at Suan Mokkh and Wat Pasukato. Six years after I first question life, I have finally found the solution which satisfied it. The solution is rather quite simple; it's happiness.

To solve social or world problem begin with the individual who comprises of it. And more importantly, how each of those individual seek and define happiness. If happiness is to "take" rather than to "give" then there is no question why there is a huge disparity, inequality, and injustice in this world. This is why my passion is to learn how to be happy and along the way help others to find true happiness one which is wholesome and beneficial to all. This is the work I cannot not do.

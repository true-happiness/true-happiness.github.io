Story

My name is Supakorn Laohasongkram. I was born in Bangkok, Thailand. I am years old. Learning about how to become a better and happier person is my passion. Ever since college, I have always want to know is what life is all about, how to rightly live, and what is the solution for making the world a better place. I've graduated from the University of Pennsylvania with degree in the Master of Applied Positive Psychology.

Commitment

Happiness is important to each one of us. But it also has a crucial role in defining our society. If happiness is to "take" rather than to "give" then there is no question why there is a huge disparity, inequality, and injustice in this world. Thus, happiness can and must be developed. So that not only will we be happier in our lives but at the same time make this world into a better place.

Over years of practice, I realize how valuable and transformative it is for one to be able to learn about happiness. I have no desire for money, fame or to prove any point. I simply know how to help and want to help. Thus, my commitment is to your happiness. My goal is to help everyone achieve True Happiness. Whether you are seeking for a greater happiness in life or in need of a helping hand. My promise is to be there to support you. This is the work I cannot not do. So do not hesitate to contact me!

Goal

My goal for this website is to offer you truth. I am not telling you to believe me. In fact, I am telling you to not believe me. Why? Because what I am giving you is merely the path. But you have to walk it so that you could see for yourself if what I say is true and whether or not it actually helps and transforms your happiness and everything around you. That is all.

Approach

We learn True Happiness like we learn how to swim. The way to truth for True Happiness is not dialogic or is it a quantitative analysis. We do not learn how to swim by debating or arguing about it nor do we take a step outside of our swimming to measure or analyse to learn how to swim. The only way to learn happiness is put the practice of True Happiness to work and see for yourself its truth.

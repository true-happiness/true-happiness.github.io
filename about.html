My Story

My name is Supakorn Laohasongkram. I was born in Bangkok, Thailand. I am years old. Learning about how to become a better and happier person is my passion. Ever since college, I have always want to know is what life is all about, how to rightly live, and what is the solution for making the world a better place.

In my search to answer these questions in life, I began studying Philosophy. I traveled, backpacked, and lived in India, France, Japan and many other countries around the world. After college, I even ordained as a Theravada Buddhist monk for one full year to experience the simplicity of happiness and explore what is life all about.

Six years after ceaselessly searching, I have finally found the answer which satisfied my questions to life. And the answer is happiness.

Happiness is important to each one of us. But it is also has a crucial in its role in defining our society. If happiness is to "take" rather than to "give" then there is no question why there is a huge disparity, inequality, and injustice in this world. Thus, happiness must can can be developed. So that not only will we be happier in our lives but at the same time make this world a better place.

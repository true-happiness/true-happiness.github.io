3. Write for True Happiness

Please email your article through contact. In addition, please also clearly provide you name, your background, and your contact information. The content of your article should...

  1. be logical e.g people can easily follow what you are trying to say.
  2. speak the Truth.
  3. written with compassion to help other people.
  4. not served your ulterior motives such as fame, money, or any egotistic fulfilment.
  5. not offend or provoke the readers in any way.

True Happiness is looking forward to your contribution! Once we received all your information, we will get back to you as soon as possible.
